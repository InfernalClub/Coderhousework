Bullet Hells are games where the player will be showered with a constant stream of bullets, hence their name, popularized in the indie scene of videogames by japan's Touhou series, created by Zun, it has a longstanding fanbase of stablished characters that span the generations of decades in computer advancement, the general story involves the main characters having to resolve an issue where they start fighting, the bullets is then a representation of the main character fighting their way through the conmotion, currently spans over 19 games over the mainline series. It's widely recognised as the first form of references when doing games of this style, as the games not only have a distinc style, they are accompanied with excelent arrangements of songs dedicated to every stage. Mechanics are explored and changed in a game by game basics. An ispiring tale given that the game is done by a one man team, Zun himself is the artist, Music composer and Programer of the whole series which spans over 20 years.
