Balance

The actual game was created with the simple premise of giving a point system that allowed users to add stats to their characters, with the

addition of an special ability and posibilities of evolving the monster, a roughly similar concept of a pokemon battle. There's only 3 stats

in the most basic itterations: HP, POWER and SPEED, with speed enabling a monster to attack more often rather than first, this was expanded

later to allow for special attacks and special defences, but for this itteration I'll stick to the 3 stat point system.
